SLOT MACHINE – GAME RULES

BASE GAME
- Grid layout: 3 rows x 5 reels
- 20 fixed paylines
- All paylines pay from LEFT to RIGHT
- Wins are multiplied by the current bet

SYMBOLS
- P1 – P6 : Regular paying symbols
- WILD    : Substitutes for all regular symbols (except SCATTER and CASHPOT)
- SCATTER : Triggers Free Spins bonus
- CASHPOT : During any spin, one or more Cashpot symbols may appear on the reels. Each Cashpot symbol value may range from x0.2 to x100 of the total bet amount. 5 or more Cashpot symbols need to appear and the sum of wins will be rewarded.

SCATTER & FREE SPINS
- 3 SCATTER symbols trigger 5 FREE SPINS.
- CASHPOT symbols appear during Free Spins.
- If 5+ CASHPOT symbols appear, the total value is paid.
- Free Spins can be retriggered.

GENERAL NOTES
- All payouts are multiplied by the current bet.
- Faulty play voids all pays and plays.
